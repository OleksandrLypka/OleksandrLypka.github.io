Website Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Oleksandr Lypka ("We", "Us", "Our") collects, uses, and discloses information when you visit and use the website hosted at oleksandrlypka.github.io ("Website").

Information We Collect

  • Cookies: We use local storage to remember your cookie consent preferences.
  • Server Logs: Our hosting provider (GitHub Pages) may collect technical data such as your IP address and browser type for security and maintenance purposes.

How We Use Information

We use collected information solely to:

  • Remember your choice regarding the cookie banner (so you don't see it every time).
  • Ensure the security and proper operation of the Website.

Choices

You can decline the use of local storage for preferences via the banner. If you decline, your preference will not be saved for future visits.

Data Retention

We retain your cookie consent preference in your browser until you clear your browser data or change your choice manually.
